Our Story

A Cozy, Quirky Slice of Rose Street

Tucked away on the famous Rose Street, Dirty Dick's has been pouring pints and plating up hearty pub grub for generations. Expect low beams, warm nooks, curious old decorations on every wall, and staff who actually know their whisky.

Whether you're after a quick pint after work, a full Scottish feast, or a dram from our extensive whisky wall, you'll find it here — served with a friendly smile and zero pretension.
